Strategy

Asset Identification

We provide investors with the opportunity to participate in carefully selected multifamily and single-family home investments. By focusing on stable, resilient markets, we aim to create consistent, long-term value while strategically positioning our assets to benefit from favorable economic and demographic trends.

We begin by targeting submarkets with strong potential for future growth. Our analysis focused on various factors like job and population increase, supply and demand trends, as well as barriers to entry. All critical factors that drive long-term property value & investment performance.

Next, we conduct a detailed market analysis to identify residential areas with desirable traits such as highly rated schools, low crime rates, local restaurants & grocery stores nearby, and access to office and retail centers. We evaluate other factors across capital and real estate markets to include treasury spends, lending terms, eviction rates, renter affordability, and CAP rate to make sound investment decisions.
